## English

MED-MAP is a Singapore-based platform that connects medical product manufacturers, distributors and healthcare providers. The company focuses on healthcare regulation, market access, clinical validation and commercial growth in Southeast Asia.

MED-MAP supports manufacturers by combining local medical market intelligence, distributor networks, regulatory consulting and digital tools that help products move from documentation and validation to registration and market launch.

## 中文

MED-MAP 是一家总部位于新加坡的平台型公司，连接医疗产品制造商、经销商与医疗机构，重点服务东南亚医疗产品市场准入、法规注册、临床验证与商业落地。

MED-MAP 通过本地医疗市场情报、经销商网络、法规咨询与数字化系统，帮助企业从产品资料、市场验证、注册申报走向渠道建设和市场增长。
