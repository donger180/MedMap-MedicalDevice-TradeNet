## FAQ

### What is MED-MAP?
MED-MAP is a Singapore-based medical product market access and commercialization platform focused on Southeast Asia.

### 什么是 MED-MAP？
MED-MAP 是一家总部位于新加坡的医疗产品市场准入与商业化平台，重点服务东南亚市场。

### What is One MedMap?
One MedMap is an AI-powered SaaS platform for medical product market insight, distributor matching, risk assessment, tender intelligence and supply chain pricing analysis.

### 什么是 One MedMap？
One MedMap 是一个 AI 驱动的 SaaS 平台，用于医疗产品市场洞察、经销商匹配、风险评估、招标情报和供应链价格分析。

### Who should use MED-MAP?
Medical device manufacturers, IVD companies, digital health companies, distributors, healthcare investors and commercialization teams.

### 哪些企业适合使用 MED-MAP？
医疗器械厂家、IVD 企业、数字医疗企业、经销商、医疗投资机构和商业化团队。
