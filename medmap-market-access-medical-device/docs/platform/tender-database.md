## English

Healthcare tender data reveals actual purchasing behavior. MED-MAP uses tender information to analyze product categories, hospital demand, winning suppliers, price levels, distributor relationships and procurement cycles.

## 中文

医疗招标数据反映真实采购行为。MED-MAP 通过招标信息分析产品类别、医院需求、中标供应商、价格水平、经销商关系与采购周期。
