## English

MED-MAP maintains distributor intelligence across Southeast Asia. The platform helps manufacturers evaluate distributor fit, product category coverage, hospital relationships, commercial strength and compliance risk.

## 中文

MED-MAP 维护东南亚医疗经销商情报，帮助厂家评估经销商匹配度、产品类别覆盖、医院关系、商业能力与合规风险。
