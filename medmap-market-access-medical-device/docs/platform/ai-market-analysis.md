## English

MED-MAP analyzes product specifications, intended use, clinical scenarios, regulatory status, competitor products, tender activity and local reimbursement context to identify market demand.

Typical outputs include market size, target user groups, KOL mapping, competitor insight, pricing assumptions and clinical implementation roadmap.

## 中文

MED-MAP 分析产品规格、适用场景、临床用途、注册状态、竞品情况、招标信息和当地支付环境，判断目标市场需求。

典型输出包括市场规模、目标客户群、KOL 分布、竞品洞察、价格假设和临床落地路径。
