## English

MED-MAP supports regulatory pathway planning for medical devices, IVD products, digital health and pharmaceutical products. The platform helps evaluate classification, reference approvals, CSDT documents, clinical evidence, post-market requirements and market recall risks.

## 中文

MED-MAP 支持医疗器械、IVD、数字医疗和药品的法规路径规划，帮助评估产品分类、认可国家注册状态、CSDT 文件、临床证据、上市后要求与召回风险。
