## English

One MedMap is an intelligent acceleration engine for medical product promotion and market expansion. It uses medical product parameters, public regulatory information, healthcare tender data and distributor intelligence to identify where a product has the strongest market fit.

The platform is designed for manufacturers, distributors, hospitals, investors and commercial teams that need evidence-based market entry decisions.

</div>

## 中文

One MedMap 是面向医疗产品推广与市场扩展的智能加速引擎。平台基于医疗产品参数、公开法规信息、医疗招标数据与经销商情报，帮助企业识别产品与市场需求的最佳交汇点。

平台适合医疗产品制造商、经销商、医院、投资机构及商业团队，用数据支撑市场进入决策。
