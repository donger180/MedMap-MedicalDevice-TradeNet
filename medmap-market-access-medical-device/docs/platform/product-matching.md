title: Product-to-Market Matching / 产品与市场匹配
description: Automated matching between medical products, clinical needs, hospitals and distributors.
---


## English

One MedMap maps a medical product to relevant clinical departments, hospital types, procurement channels and potential distributor profiles. This helps manufacturers identify the 20% of customers that may generate most of the commercial impact.

## 中文

One MedMap 将医疗产品与相关临床科室、医院类型、采购渠道及潜在经销商画像进行匹配，帮助企业根据 80/20 法则定位最重要的核心客户群体。
