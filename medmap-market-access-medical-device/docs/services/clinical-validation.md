## English

Clinical validation helps demonstrate safety, efficacy and clinical value in the target market. MED-MAP supports hospital PI connection, KOL interview, head-to-head comparison, clinical evaluation and local trial planning.

## 中文

临床验证用于证明产品在目标市场的安全性、有效性与临床价值。MED-MAP 支持医院 PI 对接、KOL 访谈、头对头比较、临床评估和本地试用规划。
