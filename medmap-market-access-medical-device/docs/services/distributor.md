title: Distributor Matching / 经销商招商匹配
description: Match manufacturers with local medical distributors and channel partners.

## English

MED-MAP helps manufacturers find suitable distributors by evaluating product category, market coverage, hospital relationships, technical support capacity, logistics capability and compliance risk.

## 中文

MED-MAP 通过产品类别、市场覆盖、医院关系、技术支持能力、物流能力与合规风险等维度，帮助厂家寻找合适经销商。
