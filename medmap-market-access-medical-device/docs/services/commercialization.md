## English

Commercialization is not only registration. MED-MAP connects market study, clinical validation, regulatory strategy, pricing, distributor partnership, tender participation, training and post-market support.

## 中文

商业化不仅是注册。MED-MAP 将市场调研、临床验证、法规策略、定价、经销商合作、招标参与、培训与上市后支持打通。
