## English

MED-MAP provides end-to-end market access services for medical product launches in Southeast Asia:

1. Market assessment  
2. KOL collaboration and clinical validation  
3. Regulatory compliance and product registration  
4. Distribution network setup  
5. Market entry strategy and launch support
