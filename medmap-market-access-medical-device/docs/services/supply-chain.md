## English

MED-MAP estimates the price dynamics across the medical supply chain: factory cost, distributor price, hospital procurement price and patient-level cost. This supports pricing strategy, margin planning and tender positioning.

## 中文

MED-MAP 推断医疗供应链中的价格动态：工厂成本、经销商价格、医院采购价与患者端价格，支持定价策略、利润规划和招标定位。
