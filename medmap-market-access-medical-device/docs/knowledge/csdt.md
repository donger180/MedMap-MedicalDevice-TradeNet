## English

CSDT refers to the ASEAN Common Submission Dossier Template. It normally includes executive summary, essential principles checklist, device description, design verification and validation summary, clinical and preclinical evidence, labeling, risk analysis and manufacturer information.

## 中文

CSDT 是东盟医疗器械通用申报资料模板，通常包括执行摘要、基本原则符合性清单、器械描述、设计验证与确认摘要、临床与临床前证据、标签、风险分析和制造商信息。
