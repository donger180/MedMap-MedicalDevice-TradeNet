## English

A typical Southeast Asia market entry roadmap includes:

1. Confirm product registration in the home country  
2. Prepare product documents and clinical evidence  
3. Conduct market research and competitor study  
4. Complete due diligence and product validation  
5. Start local clinical validation where needed  
6. Prepare regulatory submission  
7. Build distributor and hospital channels  
8. Launch with pricing, training and tender strategy  

## 中文

典型东南亚市场进入路线图包括：

1. 确认产品已完成本国注册  
2. 准备产品资料与临床证据  
3. 开展市场调研与竞品分析  
4. 完成产品尽职调查与验证  
5. 必要时启动本地临床验证  
6. 准备法规注册申报  
7. 建立经销商与医院渠道  
8. 结合价格、培训与招标策略完成上市
