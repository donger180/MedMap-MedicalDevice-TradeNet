## English

To find the right distributor, evaluate category fit, hospital access, sales coverage, technical support, tender experience, regulatory capability, compliance record and financial stability.

## 中文

寻找合适经销商时，应评估产品类别匹配、医院准入能力、销售覆盖、技术支持、招标经验、法规能力、合规记录和财务稳定性。
