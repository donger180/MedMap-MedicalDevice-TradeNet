## English

MED-MAP pricing analysis estimates the product price chain from factory cost to distributor price, hospital procurement price and patient-facing cost. This helps define launch price, channel margin and tender strategy.

## 中文

MED-MAP 的价格分析覆盖工厂成本、经销商价格、医院采购价和患者端费用，帮助企业制定上市价格、渠道利润和招标策略。
