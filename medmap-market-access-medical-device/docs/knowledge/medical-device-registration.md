## English

Medical device registration usually requires device classification, quality management proof, essential principles checklist, declaration of conformity, device description, labels, risk analysis, manufacturer information, clinical evaluation and preclinical validation documents.

## 中文

医疗器械注册通常需要产品分类、质量管理体系证明、基本原则符合性清单、符合性声明、器械描述、标签、风险分析、制造商信息、临床评价和临床前验证文件。
