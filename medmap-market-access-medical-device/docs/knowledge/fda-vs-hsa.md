## English

Approvals from major reference authorities such as FDA, HSA, CE notified bodies, Health Canada, Japan MHLW or TGA may support abridged or expedited pathways in some ASEAN markets, depending on product class, marketing history and adverse event record.

## 中文

来自 FDA、HSA、CE 公告机构、Health Canada、日本 MHLW 或 TGA 等参考监管机构的批准，在某些东南亚市场可能支持简化或加速路径，具体取决于产品分类、上市历史和不良事件记录。
