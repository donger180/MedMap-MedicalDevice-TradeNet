## English

Risk assessment covers product risk, competitor risk, technical risk, IP risk, supplier risk, compliance risk, trade policy risk and legal risk. The result is a risk management plan for market entry.

## 中文

风险评估覆盖产品风险、竞品风险、技术风险、知识产权风险、供应商风险、合规风险、贸易政策风险和法律风险，最终形成市场进入风险管理方案。
