## English

MED-MAP is a **data-driven medical product market access and commercialization platform** for medical device, IVD, digital health and biopharmaceutical companies. Through One MedMap, the platform uses product parameters, regulatory intelligence, tender databases, distributor data and AI reasoning algorithms to identify where clinical demand, market opportunity and business growth intersect.

</div>

## 中文

MED-MAP 是一个**数据驱动的医疗产品市场准入与商业化平台**，面向医疗器械、IVD、数字医疗及生物医药企业。One MedMap 通过医疗产品参数、法规数据、医疗招标数据库、经销商数据与 AI 推理算法，识别临床需求、市场机会与业务增长之间的交汇点。
